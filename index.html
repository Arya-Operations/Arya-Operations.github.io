What is Arya?

Arya is an advanced anticheat for Minecraft: Java Edition. Using an anticheat helps get rid of cheaters. And unlike other anticheats Arya comes in at $10

Checks

Arya has over 30 checks with more to come! All custom made by the Arya team or trusted developers.

Highly Customizable

Arya has a huge range of settings and messages you can change and presets to make Arya seem like other Anticheats such as Hypixel Watchdog and many more. Arya is able to be customized to your needs.

Active Development

We are constantly trying to improve Arya by adding new features and checks. Our goal is to develop the perfect anticheat at a reasonable price.

Reasonable Price

We think quality does not have to be expensive and that is why we are offering Arya for $10. No monthly fees and no other tiers. Unlike other anticheats.

FAQ

Is Arya safe to use?

Yes, it is safe to use LiquidBounce. We can guarantee that it does not contain any viruses, adware, crypto miners or other junkware. Also, LiquidBounce is open source. That means the source code is freely available. So if we ever violate our own statutes, it would be noticed immediately.

Is there a test server?

Sadly no. But we might be able to make a test server in the future with enough donations ! so please donate.

How do I update the anticheat?

Currently you will get an email from Arya's developers with the subject "A New Version Of Arya Is Ready!" with a link to download the update. in the future Arya will check with a server and say on launch that there is a new version of Arya .
